FAQ
Questions we get asked a lot.
What does the cohort model mean for my project?
+
It means we only take on three clients at a time. Your project gets our full creative energy — no context-switching, no waiting in a queue of 40 other brands.
How long does a typical project take?
+
Most projects run 4–8 weeks depending on scope. Standard packages are typically 4–5 weeks, Growth runs 6–7 weeks, and Scale projects can take up to 8 weeks.
Do you work with clients outside your area?
+
Absolutely. Everything we do is remote-friendly. We've worked with brands across the country — all you need is a Zoom link and a vision.
What if I need changes after launch?
+
Every package includes post-launch support (30–90 days depending on your plan). After that, we offer maintenance retainers or one-off update packages.
Can I upgrade my package after we start?
+
Yes — if you start with Standard and realize you need the full Growth experience, we can adjust scope and pricing mid-project. We're flexible.
What do I need to have ready before we start?
+
Just a clear idea of what your brand does and who it serves. We handle the rest — content strategy, copy direction, design, and development. A logo helps but isn't required.
